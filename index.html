About Us

The textile clothing company "WEAR & GO" was founded in 1992. At that time the main activity of the company was the production of jeans, shirts and jackets. In addition to supplying the domestic market, the company exported 25% of the production to the regional market. The company developed over the years and now has about 50 employees with potential for future growth. It has achieved the fulfillment of some standards known as ISO 9001. In recent years the company has increased its product line by increasing the production of cotton knitwear. From 2017 our company has started exporting abroad.
